Harvard Property Storymap

A reusable scroll-driven map for tracing campus landholdings, planning documents, neighborhood conflict, and expansion over time.

Story controls

Crimson parcels are active holdings in the selected year. Dark parcels are former holdings that had already been sold. Use the slider manually, or let each scroll step update the year for you.

What to edit first

  1. data/properties.geojson for parcels and dates
  2. data/overlays.geojson for chapter-specific plans, routes, or highlighted zones
  3. assets/js/config.js for chapter text, camera moves, and media
  4. assets/images/ for archival images and comparisons

This version is meant to be maintainable in GitHub, so the map behavior is driven by config and GeoJSON rather than hardcoded chapter logic.
